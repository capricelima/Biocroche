Bio Crochê, amigurumi's creation

The term amigurumi refers to the Japanese art of producing, using knitting and crochet techniques, inanimate dolls.

According to Japanese tradition, “amigurumi” (“ami” which means knitted and “nuigurumi” which means stuffed animal) have a “soul”, which turns them into a companion and confidant for their owner, providing protection and comfort and a friend for life.
