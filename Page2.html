ABOUT ME

NATURE LOVER

My first academic degree was Biological Sciences, and from then on I fell in love with nature and animals. Initially the idea of Bio Crochê was to make animals and elements of nature into amigurumis, hence the name 'BIO'.

OTHER CREATIONS

Over time and with practice, I began to create other types of amigurumis, based on films, cartoons, as well as replicas of pets (dogs and cats), people, in short, creating an infinite number of characters, depending on the customer request.
